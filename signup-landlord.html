Welcome to Rent Capital, a digital platform provided by Rent Capital Technologies Ltd, a registered company in Nigeria. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application, website, and services (“Services”).

By using our App, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not use our Services.

1. Introduction

Rent Capital is a property management and rental payment platform designed to make renting easier, safer, and more flexible for tenants, landlords, property managers, and property management companies. We facilitate features such as:

  • Rent payment scheduling and tracking
  • Auto-debit savings for rent payments
  • Shared credit wallets
  • Internal communication
  • Maintenance and repair notifications
  • Managerial and tenant permission settings
  • Real-time property and tenant status dashboards

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service.

a. Personal Information

When you create an account or use features of our App, we may collect:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Residential or Business Address
  • Gender and Date of Birth (for verification)
  • National ID or BVN (where applicable)
  • Profile photos or logos
  • Emergency contacts

b. Financial and Transaction Data

To facilitate payments and savings, we collect:

  • Bank Account Information
  • Debit Card or Payment Method Details
  • Wallet Credit Activity (sent and received)
  • Payment Records
  • Scheduled Auto-Debit Preferences
  • Escrow account payment tracking

c. Tenant-Landlord Relationship Data

  • Tenancy start and end dates
  • Rental property address and type
  • Rent amounts and service charges
  • Maintenance or repair requests
  • Payment receipt logs
  • Reason for tenant departure (requested via token, with tenant consent)

d. Device & Usage Data

To improve app performance and user experience:

  • IP Address
  • Device Type, Operating System
  • App Version
  • Date and time of use
  • Usage logs and error/crash data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Process rent payments, savings, and remittances
  • Track rent history and provide receipts
  • Enable property owners to monitor apartments and assign managers
  • Allow sharing of credit within app wallets
  • Generate rental reports and analytics
  • Notify users of upcoming rent, maintenance needs, or service charge deadlines
  • Send internal messages, alerts, and announcements
  • Prevent fraud and ensure compliance with policies

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We collect and process your data based on:

  • Your consent (when you sign up or agree to specific features)
  • The performance of a contract (e.g., rental agreements)
  • Compliance with legal obligations (e.g., financial regulations)
  • Our legitimate interest (e.g., enhancing user experience, fraud prevention)

5. Account Linking & Deletion Policy

In cases where a tenant’s account is connected to a landlord or property manager:

  • Tenants cannot unilaterally delete or unlink such accounts without prior notice and mutual consent.
  • Both tenant and landlord must acknowledge and agree to terminate such a connection.
  • The system ensures accountability and transparency for both parties involved.
  • Property owners may request limited background data about previous tenancies, subject to consent and token access.
